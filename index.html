Research Summary

My primary research focuses on leveraging big data analytics and advanced machine learning models to identify and predict the biological activities of various compounds, including their toxicity and pharmacological effects.

Research Interests

  • Multimodal machine learning for drug discovery and toxicology
  • High-throughput screening data analysis and integration
  • Multi-omics data fusion and predictive modeling
  • QSAR (Quantitative Structure-Activity Relationship) modeling
